# Robotics Mastery Checklist

Welcome to the definitive review guide for the **Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics** textbook. Use this checklist to track your progress across all five modules.

---

## 🏗️ Module 1: Foundations of Physical AI
- [ ] **1.1 What is Physical AI?**: Understand the crossover between robotics and Embodied Intelligence.
- [ ] **1.2 Sensors & State Estimation**: Know your IMUs, LiDARs, and Depth Cameras.
- [ ] **1.3 Simulation Basics**: Proficiency in Isaac Sim or Gazebo.

## 🦵 Module 2: Hardware & Mechanics
- [ ] **2.1 Kinematics & Dynamics**: Solve forward and inverse kinematics for a 7-DOF arm.
- [ ] **2.2 Actuation & Control**: Distinguish between BLDC motors, Quasi-direct drives, and Hydraulics.
- [ ] **2.3 Locomotion & Balance**: Understand ZMP (Zero Moment Point) and MPC for walking.

## 💾 Module 3: Software & Perception
- [ ] **3.1 ROS 2 Concepts**: Nodes, Topics, Services, and Actions.
- [ ] **3.2 Control Stack**: Implementing a PID or LQR controller.
- [ ] **3.3 Perception**: Real-time object detection and 3D SLAM.

## 🚀 Module 4: Deployment & Scaling
- [ ] **4.1 Digital Twins**: Syncing real hardware with simulation.
- [ ] **4.2 Benchmarking**: Measuring latency, torque-to-weight ratio, and MTBF.
- [ ] **4.3 Responsible Deployment**: Ethics of human-robot interaction.

## 🔮 Module 5: Future of Robotics
- [ ] **5.1 Emerging Research**: Foundation Models for robotics (VLA).
- [ ] **5.2 Industry Trends**: The state of the Humanoid Race in 2025.
- [ ] **5.3 Career Paths**: Roles in Perception, Controls, and Hardware Design.

---

### 🎓 Final Outcome
By completing this checklist, you are ready to architect the next generation of autonomous humanoid systems.
