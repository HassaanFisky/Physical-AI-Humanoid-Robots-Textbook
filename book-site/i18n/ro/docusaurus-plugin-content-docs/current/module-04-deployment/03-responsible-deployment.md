# Responsible Deployment

![Human and Robot Collaboration](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1485827404703-89b55fcc595e?w=1200&h=600&fit=crop)

> **Point ki Baat:** Taqatwar robots barri zimmedari mangte hain. Yeh chapter Physical AI systems ki ethics, safety aur social asraat ke baare mein hai.

## Introduction: With Great Power...

Humanoid robot koi aam app nahi hai. Yeh physical dunya mein harkat karte hain, jis ka matlab hai ke yeh logon ko nuksan pohancha sakte hain. Is liye responsible deployment koi choice nahi hai, balkay aik farz hai.

---

## Safety Standards (Hifazati Usool)

### ISO Standards
Robotics mein kuch International rules set hain:
- **ISO 10218**: Industrial robots ke liye.
- **ISO/TS 15066**: Insaanon ke sath kaam karne wale robots ke liye.
- **ISO 13482**: Personal care robots ke liye.

---

## Risk Assessment (Khatrat ka Andaza)

Robot ko deploy karne se pehle humein yeh risks dekhne hote hain:
1. **Mechanical**: Kya robot kisi ko crush kar sakta hai?
2. **Electrical**: Short circuit ya aag ka khatra.
3. **Software**: Agar code mein ghalti ho jaye toh robot kya kare ga?
4. **Cyber**: Kya koi isay hack kar sakta hai?

---

## Ethical Questions (Ikhlaqi Sawalat)

1. **Jobs**: Kya yeh robot insaanon ki jagah le le ga?
2. **Privacy**: Kya yeh robot logon ka data record kar raha hai?
3. **Responsibility**: Agar robot se ghalti ho jaye toh zimmedar kaun hoga?

---

## Aham Nukat

:::note Khulasa (Summary)

1. **Safety** par kabhi compromise na karein.
2. **ISO standards** ko follow karna lazmi hai.
3. **Risk assessment** hamesha pehle karein.
4. **Ethics** ko hamesha dhyan mein rakhein.
5. **Continuous monitoring** karte rahein.
   :::
