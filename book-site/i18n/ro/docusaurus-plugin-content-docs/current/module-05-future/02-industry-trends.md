# Industry Trends

![Robotics Industry](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1518770660439-4636190af475?w=1200&h=600&fit=crop)

> **Khulasa:** Humanoid robotics ab sirf khwab nahi raha. Tesla, Figure AI, aur Boston Dynamics jaisi companies ab isay market mein lane ki race mein hain.

## Introduction: The Humanoid Race

2024-2025 aik turning point sabit hua hai. Is waqt dunya ki bari tech companies billions of dollars in robots par laga rahi hain. Estimate hai ke 2035 tak yeh industry $38 billion tak pohanch jaye gi.

---

## Bare Khiladi (Major Players)

### Tesla Optimus
- **Focus**: Factories mein kaam karna.
- **Taqat**: Electric gaariyan banane ka experience.

### Figure AI
- **Funding**: Microsoft, OpenAI, aur NVIDIA ki support.
- **Taqat**: AI models ke zariye kamo ko samajhna.

---

## Market Segments

| Shoba | Use Case | Time |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Manufacturing** | Samaan uthana aur jorna | 2024-2025 |
| **Logistics** | Stores aur warehouses | 2025-2027 |
| **Healthcare** | Hospitals mein madad | 2027+ |
| **Consumer** | Ghar ke kaam kaaj | 2030+ |

---

## Technology Trends

1. **Electric vs Hydraulic**: Zyada tar companies ab electric motors pasand kar rahi hain kyunke yeh khamosh aur asaan hoti hain.
2. **Low Cost**: Tesla ka target hai ke robot ki price $20,000 se kam rakhi jaye takay yeh har jagah use ho sakay.

---

## Aham Nukat

:::note Khulasa (Summary)

1. **Bari Companies** market mein utar chuki hain.
2. **Factories** sab se pehle in robots ko use karein gi.
3. **Price mein kami** robots ko aam banaye gi.
4. **AI integration** sab se bara differentiator hoga.
   :::
