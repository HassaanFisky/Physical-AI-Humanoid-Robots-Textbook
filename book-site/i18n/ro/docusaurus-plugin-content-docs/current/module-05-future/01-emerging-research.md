# Emerging Research

![Modern Robotic Research](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1555255707-c07966088b7b?w=1200&h=600&fit=crop)

> **Point ki Baat:** Future yahan hai. Hum un naye research topics ko dekhein ge jo humanoid robotics ko badal rahe hain.

## Introduction: Agli Dehai (Next Decade)

Aaj ki research kal ki reality hai. Humanoid robotics ab labs se nikal kar real dunya ki taraf barh rahi hai.

---

## 1. End-to-End Learning
Pehle robots ko har movement ke liye code likhna parta tha. Ab 'Neural Networks' ke zariye robot seedha sensors se seekhta hai ke usay kaisay move karna hai.

## 2. Foundation Models
ChatGPT jaise baray models ko ab robots ke sath jora ja raha hai. Is se robot insani zaban ko behtar samajh sakte hain aur mushkil kamo ki planning kar sakte hain.

---

## 3. Soft Robotics
Sakht metal ke bajaye lachakdar (flexible) material ka use. Is se robots ka insaano ke sath kaam karna safe ho jata hai.

---

## Aham Nukat

:::note Khulasa (Summary)

1. **AI Models** robots ko samajhne ki taqat de rahe hain.
2. **Learning from Data** ab coding ki jagah le rahi hai.
3. **Soft Materials** robots ko insaano ke liye safe banate hain.
   :::
