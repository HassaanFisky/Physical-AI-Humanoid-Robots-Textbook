# Robotics Mastery Checklist

Robotics ki is definitive guide mein khush amdeed. Apni progress track karne ke liye is checklist ka use karein.

---

## 🏗️ Module 1: Foundations of Physical AI
- [ ] **1.1 Physical AI Kya Hai?**: Robotics aur Embodied Intelligence ke crossover ko samjhein.
- [ ] **1.2 Sensors & State Estimation**: IMUs, LiDARs, aur Depth Cameras ka knowledge.
- [ ] **1.3 Simulation Basics**: Isaac Sim ya Gazebo mein proficiency.

## 🦵 Module 2: Hardware & Mechanics
- [ ] **2.1 Kinematics & Dynamics**: Forward aur Inverse Kinematics ko solve karna.
- [ ] **2.2 Actuation & Control**: Motors aur Hydraulics ke darmiyan farq.
- [ ] **2.3 Locomotion & Balance**: Balance aur walking algorithms ko samjhana.

## 💾 Module 3: Software & Perception
- [ ] **3.1 ROS 2 Concepts**: Nodes, Topics, aur Services ka knowledge.
- [ ] **3.2 Control Stack**: PID ya LQR controllers ka implementation.
- [ ] **3.3 Perception**: Real-time object detection aur 3D SLAM.

## 🚀 Module 4: Deployment & Scaling
- [ ] **4.1 Digital Twins**: Simulation aur real hardware ko sync karna.
- [ ] **4.2 Benchmarking**: Performance measure karne ke tarike.
- [ ] **4.3 Responsible Deployment**: Robotics ke ethical masail.

## 🔮 Module 5: Future of Robotics
- [ ] **5.1 Emerging Research**: Foundation Models aur future tech.
- [ ] **5.2 Industry Trends**: 2025 mein Humanoid robots ki market.
- [ ] **5.3 Career Paths**: Robotics mein career ke opportunities.

---

### 🎓 Final Outcome
Is checklist ko mukammal karne ke baad, aap next generation ke autonomous systems banane ke liye tayyar hain.
